How to buy train ticket at PNROTS

STEP 1

SIGN UP if you do not have an account yet.

STEP 2

If you already have an account, go to LOG IN page and enter your account.

STEP 3

Enter your COMPLETE NAME in the form.

STEP 4

Select DATE OF TRAVEL.

STEP 5

Select DEPARTURE and ARRIVAL STATION.

STEP 6

Select your PREFERRED TIME.

STEP 7

If you are a priority person (e.g. Senior Citizen/Pregnant/Student), select your priority type. If not part of the priority person, select NONE.

STEP 8

For priority person, please provide VALID ID NUMBER that proves you belong to the priority type you selected
(e.g. PWD ID No./Student ID No./ Senior Citizen ID No.).

STEP 8.1

- For pregnant women, please provide ID number from any valid ID you have.

STEP 8.2

- For those who are not part of the priority person, you do not have to provide valid ID number.

STEP 9

After completing all the information needed, your online train ticket is completed.

STEP 10

Print the ticket and show it to the station.

ABOUT PNROTS

What is PNROTS?

The Philippine National Railways Online Ticketing System, or PNROTS, is a system that allows PNR passengers to buy train tickets online from the comfort of their own home or wherever they are. This is especially crucial during a pandemic, when we need to physically distance ourselves from people.

Because you can no longer buy a ticket at the railway station, the PNROTS eliminates large lines at the station, which may result in you being late because you spent most of your time waiting in line merely to buy a ticket. Whereas using PNROTS saves you time because it is fast and is a convenient way to purchase train tickets, as well as being a modern approach to purchasing tickets.

Philippine National Railways

The state-owned Philippine National Railways (or Pambansang Daangbakal ng Pilipinas in Filipino), commonly abbreviated as PNR, is the sole operator of the most extensive intra-island railway on Luzon, the largest island in the Philippines.
